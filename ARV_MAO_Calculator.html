Field Guide & Notes
Windows

Look for thick white trim vinyl brick on the box. Middle slider should have vinyl as well.

Metal windows = old. Large windows on the side of the house cost more.

Window count ranges: 1500 sqft or less → 10–12 windows | 1500–2000 sqft → 13–15 | 2000+ sqft → 16–18.

Electrical

Check year built. Look for pictures of the electrical socket. 3-gang sockets = updated. 2-gang = not updated.

If 75+ years old — verify if upgrades to wiring/panels were made. If <75 years old — no cost.

HVAC

Check Street View. Grates on all sides = evaporative cooler (not AC). Grates on some sides = AC unit.

If <5 years old → Existing Ducting (no cost). If 5+ years old → New Ducting ($15,000).

Roof

Check Street View. Verify if more than 5 years since last update.

If 5+ years old → enter SqFt to calculate replacement cost (uses $3/sqft estimate). If newer → select N/A.
